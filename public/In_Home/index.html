IN-HOME MASSAGE

Why Choose In-Home Massage?
Relaxing and making oneself at home is certainly much easier if the setting is the comfort of your own home. The spa experience is recreated as closely as possible with the inclusion of candles, aromatherapy, warm towels and your choice of musical selection. It is a truly wonderful feeling to bid your therapist goodnight then drift off to sleep or slip into a warm bath without having to deal with the drive home after a massage.
How Does It Work?
After having set an appointment you can expect me to arrive approximately 15 minutes before your start time to set up. Once the table, towel warmer, and candles (battery operated) are ready, we will go over your needs and expectations for the session. Lastly, I will excuse myself to wash my hands before we begin, giving you time to disrobe down to your comfort level and get under the sheets. Once the session concludes, I will leave the room to give you time to dress, after which I will break down the table, sheets, etc. and bid you farewell.
What areas are covered?
I generally provide service within the Orange County and South Los Angeles County. Service to other areas can be considered and accomodated for an additional $25 charge per session for travel and time costs and a session length minimum of 90 minutes must be met.
How much does it cost?
  • 60 minutes - $85
  • 90 minutes - $110
  • 120 minutes - $135
What days/times are available?
These appointments will generally be fulfilled during evening hours. Due to the amount of potential variability in my schedule, I cannot give set days of availability. I recommend that you get in contact with me and let me know what days and times you'd like to set the appointment for and I will be happy to accomodate you as best as I can.
