ABOUT

Hi, I'm Ivan

I'll be your set of hands today

First of all, welcome to my website and thank you so much for giving me the opportunity to potentially be a part of the wellness sphere of your life. If I already am your massage therapist then I want you to know that I appreciate you dearly and hope that you enjoy learning a bit more about me as a person. So where to start?

I never thought I'd be a massage therapist oddly enough. I went to the University of California, Riverside and studied philosophy with aspirations of becoming an attorney. One night while studying my LSAT book I realized "...I don't think I like this at all". The ensuing "What Now?" generated by that event led me to try on a slew of different potential career hats after college. None of those hats seemed to fit well, until I came across an ad for a massage school in Santa Ana called National Holistic Institute.

Soon after that I found myself at the school speaking to a representative who asked me if I'd like to tour the place; a question to which I promptly said no. I knew if I went on a tour I would have more opportunity to reason myself out of a decision my intuition had already made for me. My brain wouldn't get in my way this time.

Since graduating, in every setting I find myself in (spa, post-op recovery center, luxury hotels, etc...); I have had one goal in mind - to be the best massage therapist my clients have ever experienced. I am constantly fine tuning my technique and studying new variations to ensure I am of the utmost service to everyone who sees me. Ultimately, I'd like to travel to the Massage World Championships (yes that exists, haha) held in Copenhagen and not only compete in the competition, but win it.

When I'm not massaging, you can most likely bet on finding me engaged in at least one of the following activities: playing hockey, training for and competing in Spartan Races, walking my dog Fifi, building websites from the ground up (like the one you're on right now), reading up on some arcane philosophy books that myself and perhaps 5 others humans on earth find interesting, telling a corny dad joke or trying out some stand up comedy. I also speak two other languages in Spanish and Croatian.

I hope you've enjoyed getting to know me and my story a little bit. It would be my privilege to be your massage therapist from this day forth. I promise that you'll be in good hands (pun shamelessly intended).

Be Well,
Ivan
