IN-HOME MASSAGE

Embrace the Comfort of In-Home Massage

Experience the ultimate relaxation in the most comfortable setting possible: your own home. I bring the essence of a spa experience directly to you, complete with soothing candles, customized aromatherapy, warm towels, and your preferred musical ambiance. Imagine concluding a deeply relaxing massage session by simply drifting into sleep or unwinding in a warm bath, all without the need to travel back home.

What To Expect for Massage in Your Own Sanctuary

Upon scheduling an appointment, I'll arrive at your home about 15 minutes early to set up the perfect massage environment. This setup includes a professional massage table, towel warmer, and ambient candles (battery-operated for safety). Before we begin, we'll have a brief consultation to understand your specific needs and preferences. After which, I'll step aside to wash my hands, giving you privacy to settle in comfortably.

Serving Your Local Area

My services are primarily available in Orange County and South Los Angeles County. For locations beyond these areas, a nominal additional fee of $50 is applied to each session, accounting for extra travel and time. Please note that a minimum session length of 90 minutes is required for these extended areas.

Transparent Pricing

Flexible Scheduling for Your Convenience

I generally offer appointments during the evenings. My schedule is dynamic, so I encourage you to reach out with your preferred days and times. I will strive to accommodate your requests to the best of my ability.
