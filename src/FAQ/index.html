Frequently Asked Questions

*Updated to cover concerns regarding Covid-19

Do I have to wear a face covering?
Face coverings are required to enter Spa Wasted. Clients may remove their face covering once they enter the treatment room. I will continue to have a face covering on for the duration of your visit including during the performance of the massage. If you had chosen to remove your face covering once in the treatment room, I kindly ask that you replace it once you open the door to leave.
Are the treatment rooms being sanitized between client visits?
Yes, great care is being taken to thoroughly clean all treatment rooms and communal areas before and after each service by every Spa Wasted therapist. Additional buffer time has been included in the scheduling of appointments to allow for 30 mins between consecutive sessions to ensure that each room, its surfaces and equipment are sufficiently sanitized before another person comes in for their visit. It is our utmost priority to provide you with the cleanest and safest environment possible.
Are communal areas such as the waiting room and reception desk able to be used?
At this time all Spa Wasted therapists have been asked to inform our guests that these areas will unfortunately be unavailable for use. My guests are encouraged to send me a text message from their vehicle upon their arrival and will then receive a text in return when it is clear for them to enter the spa. The reception desk will also not be in use during this time.
How am I to pay for my service if the reception desk isn't being used?
Cash payments can be left in the treatment room in a provided envelope. Please let me know if you will need change beforehand so that I may have that ready for you. If you are paying with card or Venmo, simply state which is your preference prior to your arrival and I will either provide you with my Venmo ID to complete the paymente or I can email you an invoice via Square so you can pay with credit or debit card.
Will my temperature be taken?
Yes, a contactless thermometer will be used before you enter the spa to verify both you and myself are not running a fever.
Do I need to sign anything?
There will be a one page questionarre Covid-19 Client Intake Questionnaire that both client and therapist will sign which represents a mutual understanding that neither party has any presenting symptoms and have not, to their knowledge, been in contact with anyone recently who has been diagnosed positive.
